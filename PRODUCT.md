# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small business owners in Gujarat: property dealers, coaching classes, mobile shops, clinics, repair services, and local service providers. They are practical, value-conscious, and unfamiliar with technical jargon. They browse primarily on mobile. They trust people they can talk to — WhatsApp is their primary communication channel. Their goal: get more customers, look professional online, and grow without complexity.

## Product Purpose

Myco Labs sells digital growth packages to Gujarat-based small businesses: websites, catalogues, WhatsApp automation, marketplace/search growth, and simple software tools. The site exists to earn trust quickly and convert visitors to WhatsApp inquiries. Success means the business owner reads the page, understands the offer, and taps the CTA.

## Brand Personality

Bold, local, energetic. Not corporate. Not a generic SaaS landing page. Feels like it was built for Gujarat, not imported from Silicon Valley. Confident without being intimidating. Uses energy to communicate momentum, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI startup look (cream + purple + floating cards with gradients)
- Heavy dark websites with glowing elements (old Myco Labs design)
- Overcrowded SaaS homepages with 12 feature sections and endless iconography
- Finance/healthcare clinical coldness (white + teal + stock photos)
- Anything that looks like a Webflow template the user picked off a marketplace

## Design Principles

1. **Local over global** — Every design choice should feel made for Gujarat small businesses, not exported from a global SaaS template library.
2. **Bold presence, earned space** — Energetic but not noisy. Each element earns its place. Reduce rather than add when in doubt.
3. **Trust through clarity** — Clear hierarchy, readable at a glance. No confusing labels, no hidden costs, no visual complexity that creates doubt.
4. **Mobile-first conversion** — Primary CTA is WhatsApp. Design for thumb reach, fast scanning, and instant comprehension on a 6-inch screen.
5. **Show momentum, not decoration** — Motion and color communicate that Myco Labs delivers results. Neither is used for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Contrast ratio 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for UI components and large text. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations. Ensure tap targets are at least 44x44px on mobile.
