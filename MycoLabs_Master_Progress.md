## 7. A2 Operating Workflow

### Pre-Sales Setup

Required assets before serious A2 sales:

- Logo files.
- One-page About PDF.
- Price card with 3 tiers.
- Discovery questionnaire.
- Client agreement.
- Two sample catalogs.
- Leatherette portfolio folder.
- Photography kit.
- Prospect pipeline sheet.

### Sales Pipeline

Use these statuses:

`Cold -> Visited -> Interested -> Discovery done -> Proposal sent -> Negotiating -> Closed-Won / Closed-Lost`

Sort prospects by:

1. Tier.
2. Reviews descending.
3. Town.

First visit goal: earn the second meeting, not close on the spot.

### Proposal and Close

Follow-up cadence:

- Day 1 evening: send proposal.
- Day 3 morning: ask if they reviewed it.
- Day 7: call directly if no reply.
- Day 10: final WhatsApp plus offer to modify.
- Day 30: last check-in, then mark Closed-Lost.

When client says yes:

1. Send 50% advance invoice.
2. Confirm payment receipt.
3. Send signed agreement scan.
4. Send onboarding checklist.
5. Schedule plant visit within 5 days.
6. Block production days.
7. Move to Active Production.

### Production Flow

| Phase | Time |
|---|---|
| Sales, discovery, proposal, close | About 8 hrs over 1-2 weeks |
| Day 1 plant visit and content gathering | 4 hrs |
| Day 2 photo editing and content writing | 4 hrs |
| Day 3 catalog production | 6 hrs |
| Catalog revision | 1 hr |
| Day 4 website production | 5 hrs |
| Day 5 WhatsApp + GBP setup | 3 hrs |
| Day 6 delivery and handover | 2 hrs |
| 30-day support | 1 hr |
| Total | About 34 hrs |

Current staging rule:

- Before first paying client, prioritize PDF, WhatsApp Business, sales collateral, and first visits.
- Defer Myco Labs website work.
- Defer heavy HTML template work unless it directly supports a signed client or a close.

### Delivery Pack

Every A2 client should receive:

- Final catalog PDF, print and WhatsApp versions.
- Website URL and admin notes.
- Domain login credentials in sealed handover.
- WhatsApp catalog QR code.
- GBP login confirmation.
- One-page usage guide.
- One-page maintenance scope.
- 5 printed catalogs.
- Signed delivery checklist.
