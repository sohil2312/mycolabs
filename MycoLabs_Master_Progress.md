# Myco Labs Master Progress

**Updated:** 2026-05-11  
**Current focus:** Digital growth products for manufacturers across Gujarat.

## Current Positioning

Myco Labs helps Gujarat manufacturers improve buyer trust through simple digital assets:

- Websites.
- Product catalogues.
- WhatsApp bots and simple automation.
- IndiaMART improvement.
- Google Business Profile improvement.
- SEO starter work.
- Small software tools for lead tracking, quote requests, and review requests.

The public website should not position the offer as city-only. Local relationships can still help sales, but the brand message is Gujarat-wide.

## Current Product Menu

### Website Building

- `Starter Website`: one clean page with company intro, products, WhatsApp, Google Maps, and inquiry buttons.
- `Catalogue Website`: product categories, product cards, photos, specs, and quote buttons.
- `Growth Website`: catalogue website plus SEO pages, analytics, and stronger lead paths.

### Catalogue Building

- `PDF Sales Catalogue`: shareable catalogue for WhatsApp, email, and sales visits.
- `Category Catalogue`: catalogue divided by product type so buyers find items faster.
- `WhatsApp Mini Catalogue`: short mobile-friendly catalogue for quick buyer sharing.

### WhatsApp Bots & Automation

- `WhatsApp Auto Reply Bot`: answers common questions and shares catalogue links.
- `Lead Follow-up Automation`: reminders for new leads so inquiries are not forgotten.
- `Order/Inquiry Form Flow`: collects product, quantity, location, and callback request.

### Marketplace & Search Growth

- `IndiaMART Improvement`: better listings, product photos, descriptions, and inquiry flow.
- `Google Business Profile Improvement`: profile cleanup, photos, services, posts, and review path.
- `SEO Starter Pack`: page titles, descriptions, local keywords, headings, and search-ready structure.

### Simple Software Tools

- `Lead Tracker`: simple dashboard or sheet for buyer name, product, status, and next follow-up.
- `Quote Request Tool`: structured inquiry form that collects quote details.
- `Review Request Tool`: WhatsApp-ready flow to ask happy customers for Google reviews.
