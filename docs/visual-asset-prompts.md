Keep all assets aligned with the current direction: premium dark interface, emerald Myco Labs glow, manufacturers across Gujarat, simple product language, no public amounts, no city-only positioning, and no public AI messaging.

Primary inspiration direction: Awwwards-style digital engineering, using the `We Enable | Digital Engineers` reference as the closest fit. The adaptation should feel like a connected manufacturing growth system with product tabs, 3D nodes, beams, and practical service cards.
