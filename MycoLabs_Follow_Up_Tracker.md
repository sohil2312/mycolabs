## Lead Conversation Checklist

Use this simple flow when speaking with a manufacturer:

1. What products do you sell?
2. Do buyers ask for photos, specs, catalogue, or website link?
3. Do you already use WhatsApp catalogue, IndiaMART, or Google Business Profile?
4. Which product categories should buyers see first?
5. What is the best first package: website, catalogue, WhatsApp bot, search/profile improvement, or a combined package?
